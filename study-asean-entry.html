City Profile / 城市介绍
南京城市介绍 / Nanjing City Profile
南京，江苏省省会，地处长江下游中部，是中国四大古都之一，有"六朝古都、十朝都会"之称。全市面积6587平方公里，常住人口约950万。
Nanjing, capital of Jiangsu Province, is one of China's Four Great Ancient Capitals, known as the "Ancient Capital of Six Dynasties and Ten Regimes". Total area: 6,587 km², permanent population: approx. 9.5 million.
